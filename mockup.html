Aero-Hyd Pump Controller — System Test Plan v1.0
Objectives
  • Verify the ECU meets all DAL-B safety requirements under nominal and fault conditions.
  • Demonstrate full coverage of the SRS v3 normative statements.
  • Produce DO-178C objective evidence packages for stage-of-involvement audit.
Scope

System-level qualification of the redesigned ECU integrated with the reference hydraulic test bench. Software, hardware, and the ICD interface to the platform's CAN-FD bus.

Out of scope
  • Component-level certification of the off-the-shelf solenoid valves.
  • Environmental qualification (covered by the DO-160 campaign).

Two-tier strategy: requirement-based functional verification on the bench, followed by fault-injection robustness testing on the HIL rig with synthetic ICD traffic.

  • Build under test passes the developer's smoke-test suite (Build #421+).
  • Hydraulic test bench calibration certificate within the last 6 months.
